# Launch Notes

## Product Hunt first comment preparation

**Q: "Why not just use ChatGPT for this?"**

A: "Great question — we get this a lot. Three reasons: First, your dealbreakers are saved permanently — you set them once and they apply to every JD forever, no re-explaining. Second, we process 20 JDs simultaneously and rank them — that's 20 ChatGPT conversations collapsed into one. Third, the fake EM detection is pattern-matched against specific signals (coding percentage, report chain, hiring responsibility) — not a generic summary. The workflow is the product, not the AI."
